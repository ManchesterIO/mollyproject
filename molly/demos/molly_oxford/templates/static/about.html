About this site

Mobile Oxford was produced as part of the Erewhon Project, a JISC-funded project to explore the provision of location-aware applications and adaption of Weblearn (Oxford's implementation of the Sakai VLE) for use on mobile devices.

It is designed and managed by the Erewhon Team at Oxford University Computing Services.

Data usage

Using Mobile Oxford may incur a cost of data transmission from your network provider. Many providers now offer "unlimited" day, week or month passes, please check with your provider for more information.
