Accessing Mobile Oxford

What phones are supported by Mobile Oxford?

We aim to support as wide a range of phones as possible, and expect that you should have a usable experience on any phone. We test on Android, BlackBerry, iPhone, Sony Ericsson, Symbian Series 40 and 60, and Windows Mobile. However, if you find it doesn't look quite right or some part of the site doesn't work as expected, please let us know by e-mailing us.
