Mobile Oxford is a new service run for students and staff at the University of Oxford as well as the general public. Incorporating information and access to services from across the institution, Mobile Oxford is a central point to act as your guide in both the city and the University.

Features

  • Contact search

    Search the University contact directory with one-click access to e-mail or phone.

  • Library search

    Search for books and other items by author, title or ISBN. Results are displayed with the nearest first and simple maps to guide you to the library.

  • Maps

    Search for colleges, departments, restaurants, bus stops and much more. Bus stops have live arrival predictions, post boxes have collection times (where available), and University pages let you explore the relationships between them.

  • Podcasts

    Listen to any podcast published through the University's podcast service and Apple's iTunes U.

  • Webcams

    View webcams from across the city. Check the weather, the traffic, or simply wave at yourself.

  • News

    Read news from across the University to find out about events and recent developments.

  • Results releases

    Check whether your examination school has had its results released so you know when to check the Student Self-Service System.

  • Weather

    Check the latest weather reports from Oxford.

Accessing the site

  • Open your phone's web browser and head to: m.ox.ac.uk
  • Point your phone's barcode scanner at:
  • QR Code
  • Datamatrix Code
  • Instructions for setting up wireless access from your mobile device

Data usage

While this service is provided free of charge, your mobile operator may charge for the data you transfer. Please check that using this site does not incur any unexpected charges before accessing it.

Have feedback?

Mobile Oxford is a rapidly developing service, and we'd like to know what you think. If you have any comments, suggestions or feature requests you can e-mail us at mobileoxford@oucs.ox.ac.uk. Additionally, if you have any data source you'd like to see included, get in touch and we'll see what we can do.
